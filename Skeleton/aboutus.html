About Us

We split our time between Client work and own IP work dedicating the fourth week of every month to the creation of internal products. We aim to develop one internal product to a fundable position each quarter. The Management Team reviews internal products, and if they feel that there’s commercial value in them then funding will be sourced through Kickstarter. Successful Kickstarter campaigns will continue to be developed and breakout products will be spun out into new companies.

The experience we gain from these internal projects will refine our expertise in, and reputation for, the conception, development, financing and release of products. In turn this will increase the value of the work that we deliver to our clients.

Our Methods

Key methods and concepts within Strange include:

The Power Of Video

Strange recognises that the use of film, 3D and motion graphics in the creation and documentation of client and own IP projects, is vital to the development of the company’s reputation and the promotion our work.

Chargeable Products

All internal products are chargeable products. Strange does not make free services or products although we may explore freemium business models - particularly in relation to physical products.

Kickstarter Experts

At the end of their prototyping cycle (max. 1 quarter) own IP products will be evaluated by the senior team. Products that are judged to have potential will seek funding through kickstarter. As a result Strange will quickly develop and high level of expertise in the creation and execution of kickstarter campaigns.

Writing

Along with video documentation Strange recognise that In order to cement their reputation as thought and process leaders they need to share their thoughts and ideas publicly. All Strange staff are contribute to the company blog each week and Strange will endeavor to find additional outlets for this material. In addition to these posts Strange will post a ‘Monday Funday’ article and ‘That was the week that was’ article each week.

The Ideas Bank

All our own IP projects are drawn from the Strange Ideas Bank. Concepts and ideas are contributed to the bank by employees and shared internally. Once shared any other employee can fork the idea and change/alter it before committing their updated version. On the Friday before the first sprint for new internal project the team will discuss the merits and flaws of these ideas and select the next product for development.

Rapid Prototyping

Everyone at Strange has experience of working in early stage startups and they draw upon this expertise to rapidly prototype and evaluate digital and physical products, utilising technologies ranging from paper mockups through to the latest in 3D printing.
