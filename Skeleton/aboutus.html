About Us

We’ve setup Strange because we are fed up with ‘startup culture’ and the financial mechanics associated with it. We want to build transformational technologies and solve real world problems and sharing our ability to do this with clients seems like the best way for us to achieve this goal.

Alongside client work we create and develop our own IP projects, with the aim of launching at least 2 new products every year. The experience we gain from these internal projects will help us provide even more value to you!

As a team our interest lies in the collision between fact and fiction (see Clarke’s Third Law) but we’ll work on anything that sparks our interest. So whether you’re an established company that wants to explore new technologies or a startup with a fantastic idea that you’re struggling to implement, we’d love to hear form you!

Our Core Concepts

Here are some of the Key concepts that we’re building ourselves around at Strange:

Rapid Prototyping

It’s better to do, than to ponder. Everyone at Strange has experience of working in early stage startups and we will draw upon that expertise to rapidly prototype digital and physical products, utilising technologies that range from pen and paper wireframes through to the latest in 3D printing.

Own IP Projects

At the core of what we do at Strange are our own IP projects. Both an opportunity to be truly experimental AND a planned revenue stream (we aim to reach the point where at least 50% of our revenues come from Own IP Projects), we aim to release at least one internal product for sale every six months.

The Ideas Bank

All our own IP projects will be drawn from the Strange Ideas Bank. Any member of Strange can contribute an idea to the bank, these are then openly shared amongst their colleagues. These ideas can then be added to, or altered, and form the pool from which own IP projects are selected for exploration.

The Power Of Video

We recognise the power of film, 3D modeling and motion graphics, both in the ideation and creation of products, and in the effective communication of their merits.

Chargeable Products

All internal our products will be ‘premium’ products. Whilst we’re interested in exploring different types of business models Strange will not make free services or products. We’re planning to build real value into the goods that we produce so we’ll be looking to charge a real value to access them.

Crowdfunding Experts

At the end of the prototyping cycle own IP products will be evaluated by the Principal team. Products judged to have potential will seek funding for further development through crowdfunding services. Not only does this provide funding for further development it also ensures there’s an audience of customers willing to pay for the product.
